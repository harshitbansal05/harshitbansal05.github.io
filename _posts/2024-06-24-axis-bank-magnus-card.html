Travel & Stay - Magnus Credit Card

WELCOME BENEFIT
  • Choose any voucher worth INR 12,500* from below options:
    • Luxe gift card or The Postcard Hotels gift voucher or Yatra gift voucher
  • How to Avail:
  • An SMS will be sent to your registered mobile number within 3 working days upon your Magnus card activation, by making first spend transaction within 30 days of card issuance.

  • Post landing on the portal, please choose the preferred voucher.
  • Terms & Conditions:
  • Benefit is available to primary cardholder only and eligible on activation of the Magnus credit card
  • Benefit can be availed within 6 months from card creation date, if a transaction is done within 30 days
  • For flight ticket:
    • Booking should be made at least 10 days prior to the date of travel
    • The discount amount for each flight ticket is capped at Rs.12,500
    • Applicable on one-way domestic economy flight ticket
    • Flights bookings are subject to availability; Axis Bank and travel partner hold the right to determine flights applicable under this offer
  • For detailed T&C click here

Airport Concierge Service

Enjoy 8 complimentary Airport Meet & Greet services* for a smooth and hassle free airport check-in. The services includes assistance across airport processes, such as checkin, security check, immigration process and porter services.

(With effect from 20th April'24 Airport Concierge Services have been discontinued for all Magnus Customers. *For Magnus Burgundy Customers, w.e.f 20th April'24 Airport Meet & Assist Services have been revised from 8 to 4 per calendar year.)

  • How to Avail:
  • Visit our experience page, www.extraordinaryweekends.com to place the request.
  • A confirmation mail is sent to you with the visit details like meeting time, meeting point, etc.
  • Get greeted at the airport at scheduled time and place.
  • Terms & Conditions:
  • Benefit is capped to 8 complimentary Meet & Greet services in a calendar year.
  • The primary cardholder can use the services for his/her accompanying guests.
  • Bookings should be made at least 48 hours before the service time.
  • Valid for domestic and international departures/arrivals.
  • Valid at 16 airports across India.
  • Meet & Greet services are subject to availability by the service provider.
  • For Domestic Departures, service time will be 90 Mins prior to the flight departure time. For International Departure service time will be 180 Mins prior to the flight departure time. Service details along with the name of the person responsible for conducting the service will be shared 3 Hours before the service time.

Complimentary International Lounge Access

Enjoy unlimited cvomplimentary international lounge visits with 8 additional guest visits per year with priority pass card
(With effect from 20th April'24 compliementary 8 additional guest visits per year has been revised to 4 guest visits per year on Priority Pass Card.)

  • How to Avail:
  • The Priority Pass membership card will be delivered to you along with the credit card in the welcome kit
  • Swipe your Priority Pass Card at the eligible lounges to avail complimentary visits
  • Any accompanying guests will be captured in the slip handed by lounge staff
  • For eligible lounges, visit: www.prioritypass.com
  • To access select domestic lounges at Indian Airports please use your Priority Pass card. Click here for the list of domestic lounges.
  • Terms & Conditions:
  • For availing guest visits, the primary cardholder must be traveling along with the guest.
  • The Priority Pass Membership Card is linked to the credit card’s status. If the credit card is blocked for usage, the Priority Pass card will also be blocked for usage
  • It will take at least 24 working hours to unblock the Priority Pass card, once the credit card has been updated

Lifestyle & Entertainment - Magnus Credit Card

BookMyShow Benefits

Buy one ticket and get up to Rs. 500 off on the second ticket for a maximum of 5 movie/non-movie bookings in a month.
(With effect from 20th April'24 BookMyShow benefits have been discontinued for all Magnus & Magnus Burgundy Customers.)

  • How to Avail:
  • Go through the regular ticketing flow for selecting the desired movie or event and proceed to payments page
  • To avail the offer, check ‘unlock offers or apply promo codes’
  • In the search offer section, enter ‘axis bank magnus credit card offer’and select from the offers list
  • Enter your 16-digit credit card number in the box and click on 'check'
  • Congratulations! you would have successfully availed your offer
  • You will have to pay the remaining transaction amount using the same card on which you have availed the offer. your card number will auto-populate in the box. to make the payment, enter your name on the card, expiry date and cvv card details
  • Terms & Conditions:
  • Offer is valid for a maximum of 5 times in a month
  • The discount amount for each transaction is capped at Rs. 500 per transaction
  • Offer is applicable both on movie and non-movie tickets
  • Offer is applicable only on BookMyShow website and mobile App
